# Cleaning Strategy Tradeoff Analysis

## AI Role
Analyst (tradeoff enumerator)

## Prompt
Compare two data cleaning strategies for the following decision:
[INSERT DECISION]

Strategy A:
- Reduce ambiguity
- Stabilize labels and behavior

Strategy B:
- Preserve ambiguity
- Accept noisy signals

For each strategy:
1. What uncertainty is suppressed or preserved?
2. What risks become more visible?
3. What downstream failures are introduced?

Do NOT:
- choose a winner
- collapse tradeoffs
