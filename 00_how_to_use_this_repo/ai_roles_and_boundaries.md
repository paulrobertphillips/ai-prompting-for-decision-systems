# AI Roles and Boundaries

AI should never be treated as a single capability.

This repo distinguishes **three explicit AI roles**.

---

## 1. AI as Analyst

**Purpose**
- Expand the space of possibilities
- Enumerate interpretations
- Surface tradeoffs

**Allowed**
- Listing options
- Comparing strategies
- Surfacing assumptions

**Not Allowed**
- Final decisions
- Threshold selection
- Deployment approval

---

## 2. AI as Reviewer

**Purpose**
- Critique pipelines
- Identify risks
- Stress-test assumptions

**Allowed**
- Flagging failure modes
- Challenging confidence
- Identifying fragile assumptions

**Not Allowed**
- “Fixing” systems
- Quietly changing scope

---

## 3. AI as Implementer (Use Sparingly)

**Purpose**
- Execute clearly defined plans

**Allowed**
- Writing code
- Applying transformations
- Implementing approved logic

**Not Allowed**
- Making design decisions
- Choosing metrics or thresholds

---

## Guiding Rule

> When in doubt, keep AI in **Reviewer** mode.
