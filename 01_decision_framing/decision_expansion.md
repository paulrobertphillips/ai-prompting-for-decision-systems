# Decision Expansion Prompt

## AI Role
Analyst (non-authoritative, expansive)

## Prompt
You are assisting with early-stage decision framing.

Context:
- Business goal: [INSERT]
- Decision to be supported: [INSERT]
- Primary risk if wrong: [INSERT]

Please provide:
1. Multiple interpretations of the decision
2. Ways this decision could be framed too narrowly
3. Hidden assumptions that should be surfaced before proceeding

Do NOT:
- recommend models
- recommend metrics
- assume data quality
